Artist Information

My name is Kristina Rose-marie Albright and welcome to my artistry page.
I went to Flemming college for Art in Haliburton, ON. my passion has always been painting and while I still love to paint, it is harder to sell then the craft I learned from school.
Pottery has advanced my creativity and increased my passion.
I currently work as a pottery technition at the Kawartha Pottery Guild in Peterbourogh. I recently visited South Korea to learn from their pottery masters and absorb some of the skills that have been passed down thorough the generations.
I was surprising applauded for my interest as apprentaly there are fewer and fewer young adults going into art as a career and it needs to be preserved.

New Classes

The new class schedule starts Jan 18th and will be held once a week for a total of 8 sessions.
You must be at least 12 to come without an adult with you, if under that please be accompanied by someone who is at least 18 years old. There are different classes for different levels. If you don't know what your level would be then you can either sign up for the beginner and go from there or feel free to contact me and I can help you determine which class to take. Spots can fill up fast, so its first come first serve.

  • Monday 12 to 2pm (beginner) or 2 to 4pm (intermediate)
  • Wednesday 11 to 1pm (intermediate) or 2 to 4pm (advanced)
  • Friday 4 to 6pm (beginner) or 6 to 8pm (advanced)
